About Me

"Design is how I tell stories without words"

I'm David Ugochukwu Nwoko, a passionate graphic designer based in Abuja, Nigeria with over 4 years of hands-on experience crafting visual identities that leave lasting impressions.

From fintech startups to corporate brands, I bring a strategic yet artistic approach to every project — ensuring design not only looks exceptional but drives real business results.

I specialise in brand identity, print & digital design, product packaging, and social media content creation. Currently serving as an IT Support Engineer at Systemspecs Holdings Limited, I combine creative design expertise with technical proficiency.
